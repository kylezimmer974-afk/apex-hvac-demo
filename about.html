Our Story

Your home's changing. Don't just fix a system — find what's next.

Apex Heating & Cooling has served Frederick MD and the surrounding communities for over 12 years. We built this company on a simple belief: homeowners deserve honest advice, expert technicians, and work they can stand behind.

Our technicians hold NATE certification — the gold standard in HVAC credentials — and complete ongoing training on the latest systems from Carrier, Lennox, Trane, and more. We show up on time, explain exactly what we find, and never recommend a repair or replacement you don't actually need.

Whether it's an emergency repair at midnight or a planned system upgrade, Apex Heating & Cooling is the team your neighbors call first.
